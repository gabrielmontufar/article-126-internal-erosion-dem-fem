Deng et al. (2020) is included as an independent external mechanism challenge for Article 126. It is not used as calibration, A/B validation, or field validation. The source supports the need for path-aware screening because horizontal suffusion experiments under controlled vertical stress report local hydraulic-gradient nonuniformity, eroded fine-particle mass, and permeability evolution after initiation. The manuscript may claim that this evidence makes a pure global critical-gradient cutoff inadequate as the only comparator. The manuscript must not claim that the FEM-Dijkstra-DEM model predicts or is calibrated to Deng et al. (2020).
